# Remote Work Policy

## Location
- Work from anywhere
- Must overlap 3+ hours with team
- Good internet required

## Equipment
- $2,000 laptop budget
- $500 home office stipend
- $50/month co-working allowance

## Communication
- Daily standups (async)
- Weekly all-hands
- Monthly 1:1s
